The official registry of zoological nomenclature. ZooBank provides a means for authors and publishers to electronically register new nomenclatural acts, and assigns unique Life Science Identifiers (lsids) to those names.

Register a new name

All names exist within the context of a publication. To register a name, we therefore need to know where it was (or will be) published.

You may fill out the form below; save yourself time by pasting in a doi or choosing one of your recently-entered publications.
